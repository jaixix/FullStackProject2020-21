GLA University, Mathura is ranked 2 in UP and 6 in India amongst all Private B-Schools Ranking by Times B-School and ranked 1 Best Private University in UP in Engineering Survey by Dainik Jagran. The university offers undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral degrees in the discipline of Engineering, Pharmacy, Science, Education and many more.

HISTORY

In the year 1991 Shri Narayan Das Agrawal decided to fulfil the dream of his father, Late Shri Ganeshi Lal Agrawal and initiated the Sri Jagannath Prasad Ganeshi Lal Bajaj Charitable Trust Samiti in an attempt to make the holy city of Mathura a recognized destination for knowledge seekers from different walks of life.
