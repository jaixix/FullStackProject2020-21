Galgotias University (GU), Greater Noida ranked 2nd in Uttar Pradesh and 3rd in India by GHRDC Business School Survey 2019. It is also ranked among Top 10 Private University by Times B-School Ranking 2019. The University offers 62 UG, 44 PG, 25 Doctoral, 1 Certificate, 15 Diploma, and 5 Integrated courses. The university has a total of 15 schools on its campus for each specialization. GU Noida has a placement record of providing 2000+ offers to students in 5 months. More than 450 companies have visited the university for on-campus recruitment.

HISTORY

Mr. Kandarp Ashutosh is the president of Galgotias University. The institute was founded in 2011. Dhruv Galgotia is the CEO while Dr. Preeti Bajaj is the Vice Chancellor of the Foundation.
